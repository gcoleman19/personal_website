About Us

Not Your Average Granola was born due to Emme's dietary restrictions. She always struggled to find gluten free granola.

As sisters we started making delicious GF granola as a team. Our family and friends became addicted to our recipe and we thought this would be an amazing product to share with the world.

This granola is a perfect for yogurt, ice cream, or just by the handful. Our granola can be enjoyed by all - gluten free or not.

Meet the Sisters!

Emme is gluten free and loves to cook. She is the genius behind all of our mixes!

Grace runs the business side of of NYAG. She loves running our social media and website.
